  Introduction. 

  Evil Invasion is a hardcore arcade shooter with a dash of role-playing elements that takes place in the world of powerful magic, great heroes and dangerous monsters.

  You are the Hero, the Chosen One, the last hope of humanity.
  Your mission is simple: protect the world from evil hordes!
  There are two gameplay modes: Quests and Survival. Complete all quests first, then try to repel against endless evil forces in Survival!  Many spells and abilities give numerous strategies to accomplish your mission.

  You may update High Scores table using "On-Line Update" button. Internet connection and and right settings in the InetSettings are required for update. Look at the InetSetting for further details.

 

  System Requirements and Performance.

  Recommended System.
    PC with 256mb memory
    Windows XP
    1Gh system processor
    DirectX 9.0c or higher ( you can download the latest version from http://www.microsoft.com/windows/directx/ )
    Video card NVIDIA GeForce2, analog or higher (for high resolutions)
    Sound Card.


  Performance hints.
    The minimum system can be slower but you will not be able to get satisfaction from game, so you have to upgrade.
    As far as, lots of graphics content can be drawn on screen per frame, for using high resolution (1280x1024) and having smooth gameplay you should have video card class NVIDIA GeForce3, analog or higher.

  Hero.

    Hero has primary attributes, skill & spells to protect himself from evil hordes. When hero kills monsters he earns experience, when he killed enough monsters he gains level. When hero has gained level he rewards with 5 primary attribute point and 1 skill point. You can increase primary attributes and/or spells & skills. The limit on any primary attribute is 100, the limit on any skill or spell  is 10.

  Primary Attributes.

  • Strength.
      1 strength point increases by 10 health points.
      1     strength points decreases by 0.004 seconds hit recovery time. Base hero hit recovery time is 0.5 seconds. Hero become under hit recovery effect after a monster has successfully attacked him. Hero can not cast spells under hit recovery and he slowed by 20%.
  • Dexterity.
      1 dexterity point increases by 0.5 hero speed. Base hero speed is 65.
      1 dexterity point decreases by 0.4% chance to be hit. Base hero chance to be hit by melee monster attack is 100%.
  • Stamina.
      1 stamina point increases by 0.1 health point regeneration per second.
      1 stamina point increases by 0.35 hero speed.
    Intelligence.
      1 intelligence point increases by 6 hero mana capacity.
      1 intelligence point increases by 0.06 mana point regeneration per second.
  • Wisdom.
      1 wisdom point increases by 2 hero mana capacity.
      1 wisdom point increases by 0.18 mana point regeneration per second.


  Spells & Skills.

  • Spells and skills are divided into four sections respecting to the god granted power for certain magic ability.
  • Spells can be casted using mana or health. So the mana and health amount is critical for particular  spells.
  • Skills are passive abilities that are working all the time the hero posses them.
Basic Spell: Magic Missile.
Earth Spells: Prayer, Mystic Fog, Teleport, Wrath of God.
Air Spells: Sacrifice, Holy Light, Ball Lightning, Chain Lightning.
Fire Spells: Fire Ball, Heal, Meteor, Armageddon.
Water Spells: Freezing Spear, Poison Cloud, Nova, Dark Ritual.
Skills: Stone Enchanted, Telekinesis, Fire Walk, Fortuna.


 

Magic Missile.
  Slay enemies with self-guided magic missile.
  Spell is very useful because it slows monsters by 75% for short period of time.
Prayer.
  Offer some blood to your god and receive some mana for your deals.
  Spell allows to trade health for getting mana.
Mystic Fog.
  Surround your enemies with mystic fog, slowing them and
  receive some time to decide what to do with them.
Teleport.
  Instantly teleports you to desired destination.
  Spell can save your life.
Stone Enchanted.
  Turn to stone everyone who touches you forever.
  Skill allows to kill monsters with melee attack for free, without taking any damage.
Wrath of God.
  Send wrath of your god to those who tries to kill you.
  Spell allows to kill nearby monsters.
Telekinesis.
  Take items on a distance using strong will.
  Skill allows to take items by move cursor over them and hold it during some time.
Sacrifice.
  Sacrifice blood to ask the God kill anyone by your choice.
  Spell allows to kill any monster instantly, there is not protection from this spell.
Holy Light.
  Even the strongest demons can not see holy light without harm to there health.
  Spell allows to kill monsters with holy light spears.
Ball Lightning.
  Flying energy ball that strikes nearby monsters.
  Running near these balls can make life easier.
Chain Lightning.
  Release a lightning spear, that deal lots of damage.
  Spell is very powerful.
Fire Ball.
  Burn monsters with fire.
  Nearby monsters will suffer from fire too.
Fire Walk.
  The ground burns under you.
  Skill allows to burn monsters pursuit you. 
Heal.
  Heals wounds over 3 seconds.
  Spell with high level certainly can prevent death.
Meteor.
   Send cosmos wrath on evil ones.
  Spell do lots of damage to target area.
Armageddon.
  Skies rain fire destructing everything.
  Spell allows kill many monsters on whole map randomly.
Freezing Spear.
   Pierce and freeze monsters with magical ice spear.
  Spell allows to freeze monsters.
Fortuna.
  Increase chance to find bonus items.
  Sometimes bonuses can be very critical to survival.
Poison Cloud.
  Continuously damages monsters entered in area.
  Spell is useful when lots of monsters gathers in small area.
Nova.
  Damage monsters with freezing waves.
  Spell is very useful as far as it freezes monsters.
Dark Ritual.
  Banish monsters from existence. Spell does not affect Mummy, Diablo.
  Spell allows to instantly kill monsters in target area with some probability.

 

  Monsters.

  • There are 7 monster types.
  • When the hero growths in levels monsters growth too. Each newly spawned monster has the same level as hero.
  • The table of monster parameters describes how monster parameters growth with levels.
  • All tables are given for Survival mode, that allows only Hardcore difficulty.
  • On Normal difficulty monsters have less hit points by 40% and are slower by 40% (base speed and speed per level are less by 40%).
  • On Nightmare difficulty monsters have less hit points by 20% and are slower by 20% (base speed and speed per level are less by 20%).
  • In Quest mode hero earns two times less experience from monsters.
  • In Survival mode after 40 level monsters obtain additional +10% to Hit Points and +%10 to Speed for each next level. For example, at 60 level they get +200% Hit Point and +200% to Speed.
Troll.
  Weak melee fighter.
Spider.
  Tough though stupid insect.
Mummy.
  Fast warrior with range attack.
Blood Demon.
  Exceptional melee fighter. When demon starts to pursuit its speed increases by 40%.
Bear.
  Strong melee fighter.
Reaper.
  Strong warrior with magic range attack. Attacks with 3 flames.
Diablo.
  Diablo does not fight with the hero himself. Instead of this, Diablo summons different monsters around the hero.
  Probability of summoning: 5% Reapers, 10% Blood Demons, 15% Bears, 20% Mummies, 25% Spiders, 25% Trolls.

  Enchanted Monsters.

  • Each monster can be enchanted with probability 15%.
  • Enchanted monster has auras protected them from certain magic spells.
  • Enchanted monster always has magic damage.
  • Magic damage burns also mana in half amount as life.
  • Enchanted monsters gives four times more experience.

  Damage Types.

  • Physical damage is dealt by non-enchanted monsters.
  • Magic damage is dealt by enchanted monsters and Reaper flames.

  Protective Auras.

  Protects from Magic Missile.
Earth Aura.
  Protects from Mystic Fog, Wrath of God.
Air Aura.
  Protects from Holy Light, Ball Lightning, Chain Lightning.
Fire Aura.
  Protects from Fire Ball, Meteor, Armageddon.
Water Aura.
  Protects from Freezing Spear, Poison Cloud, Nova, Dark Ritual.


 

  Bonuses.

  • There are 12 bonus items that can be dropped by monsters after they died.
  • Basic probability of dropping is 10%.
  • Skill Fortuna can increase probability of dropping.
  • All items are available for picking up during 10 seconds before they disappear.
  • Continued effects Haste, Physical Shield, Quad Damage, Free Spells, Invulnerability last over 15 seconds.

  Bonus Items.

Health Potion.

  Restores 50% of health over 5 seconds.
Mana Potion.

  Restores 50% of mana over 5 seconds.
Rejuvenation Potion.

  Restores 50% of health and 50% of mana immediately.
Boots of Haste.

  Increases speed by 40%.
Tome of Experience.

  Gives 20% of experience required for next level.
Magic Shield.

  Decreases magic damage by 80%.
Physical Shield.

  Decreases physical damage by 80%.
Quad Damage.

  Spells inflict four times more damage.
Free Spells.

  Allows cast spells without mana or health cost.
Attribute Point.

  Gives five attribute points permanently.
Skill Point.

  Gives one skill point permanently.
Invulnerability.

  Gives complete invulnerability.

 

  Bonus items value by dropping frequency.

Common (40%): Health Potion, Mana Potion.
Uncommon (30%): Rejuvenation Potion, Haste.
Unique (15%): Tome of Experience, Magic Shield.
Rare (10%): Physical Shield, Quad Damage, Free Spells.
Exceptional (5%): Attribute Point Item, Skill Point Item, Invulnerability.

 

  Strategies FAQ.

Q: Why monsters are faster then hero? They catch me and kill!
A: Increase your dexterity at least by 1 point at each level. Hero will be as fast as monsters.
     Put more points into Fortuna skill to have more Haste bonus items.      

Q: Monsters are too strong - they kill me instantly! What to do?
A: Increase your strength at least by 1 point at each level. Hero will have more health.
     Cast Heal when health points are running low.
     Put more points into Fortuna skill to have more Health bonus items.

Q: I very often have no mana to cast spells and kill them! What to do?
A: Increase your intelligence to have more mana capacity.
      Increase wisdom to have much faster mana regeneration.
      Put more points into Fortuna skill to have more Mana bonus items.
      Cast Prayer. Then cast Heal.
      Combine all mentioned above.
